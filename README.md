## Navigating ClubManager

### Getting Started

1. **Visit the Landing Page**: When you first arrive, you'll see our gorgeous hero section with feature highlights, animated backgrounds, and a live activity preview.

2. **Sign Up or Login**: Click "Get Started Free" to create your account, or "Explore Demo" to see the platform in action. Use email/password or mock Google sign-in.

3. **Explore the Dashboard**: After logging in, you'll land on your personalized dashboard showing:
   - Total members, events, and engagement stats
   - Recent community activity in real-time
   - Quick action cards for common tasks

### Main Navigation (Authenticated Users)

Use the **collapsible sidebar** on desktop (or hamburger menu on mobile) to access:

- **Dashboard**: Your command center
- **Members**: Manage your community roster
- **Events**: Plan and track events
- **Announcements**: Post and view updates
- **Files**: Upload and organize documents
- **Analytics**: View performance metrics
- **Settings**: Customize your experience

### Public Pages

Accessible to all visitors:

- **Pricing**: Compare plans and pricing tiers
- **Roadmap**: See what's coming next
- **About**: Learn about our mission and team
- **Blog**: Read community management insights
- **Careers**: Explore job opportunities

### Top Navigation Bar

- **Logo**: Return to homepage
- **Search**: Find members, events, or content (when authenticated)
- **Notifications**: Stay updated with activity (when authenticated)
- **Profile Avatar**: Access settings and account options (when authenticated)
- **Mobile Menu**: Hamburger icon on smaller screens
